TRAVEL SAFE

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Travel Safe ("the App") is operated by an individual developer ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use Travel Safe on Android devices. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account via Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and Google profile photo. This data is stored securely in our database.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device location to show relevant emergency zones and alerts in your area. We do not continuously track or store your precise location history.
  • Device Information: We collect your device's push notification token to deliver emergency alerts. We also collect your country or region preference for filtering relevant notifications.
  • Emergency Contacts: If you use the I'm Safe feature, you select contacts from your device. We do not store your contacts on our servers. Contact information is used solely to send a one-time SMS notification from your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymised data about how you interact with the App, such as which regions you monitor and how often you open the App, to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver real-time emergency alerts and zone intelligence relevant to your monitored regions
  • To authenticate your account securely via Google OAuth
  • To send push notifications to your device when emergency events occur in your monitored regions
  • To personalise your experience including region preferences and theme settings
  • To improve the App through anonymised usage analytics
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on Supabase, a secure cloud database platform. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address listed in Section 10.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database services and Google Firebase for push notifications. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by appropriate data protection agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Safety: We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves or others.

6. International Data Transfers

Travel Safe is operated from the United States and is intended for global use. If you are located outside the United States, including in the European Economic Area (EEA), your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries. By using the App, you consent to such transfer.

For users in the EEA, we process your data based on the following legal grounds: performance of a contract (providing the App service), your consent (location access and notifications), and our legitimate interests (improving the App and maintaining security).

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Travel Safe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the effective date above. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Email: support@travelsafeapp.com

11. Disclaimer on Emergency Data Accuracy

Travel Safe provides emergency intelligence information sourced from government advisories, humanitarian organisations, and verified field reports. While we strive to ensure accuracy and timeliness, emergency situations are dynamic and information may not always reflect real-time conditions. Travel Safe is an informational tool and should not be used as the sole basis for emergency decisions. Always follow guidance from local authorities and emergency services.
