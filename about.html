Our Story

Built for the traveller who deserves better.

Emergency intelligence has always existed — it just wasn't accessible to everyone. Travel Safe changes that.

Why Travel Safe exists

Large organisations — corporations, governments, major NGOs — have always had dedicated teams monitoring government advisories, embassy security alerts, and official emergency feeds around the clock. Individual travellers had no equivalent.

Most people relied on news alerts that arrived after a situation had already escalated, or government travel advisories that were difficult to find and hard to read on a phone. The information was always out there — it just wasn't accessible, consolidated, or delivered in a useful way.

Travel Safe was built to close that gap. The same publicly available government advisories, embassy alerts, and verified emergency intelligence — consolidated, mapped, and delivered directly to your device.

Every person travelling the world deserves the same situational awareness that large organisations take for granted. Safety intelligence should not be a luxury.

What we believe

🎯
Accuracy first

Every alert shows its source. We never publish intelligence we cannot attribute to a credible origin.

⚡
Speed matters

In an emergency, minutes count. Travel Safe is built to get verified intelligence to you before the situation escalates.

🔒
Privacy by design

We collect only what we need. We never sell your data. Your safety information is yours.

🌍
Truly global

Coverage across 195 countries from day one. Whether you're in Bangkok or Bogotá, Travel Safe is watching.

Who Travel Safe is for

Travel Safe was designed for anyone whose safety depends on knowing what's happening around them. That includes solo travellers navigating unfamiliar regions, expats living abroad who want to monitor both their home country and where they live now, families wanting to know their loved ones overseas are safe, NGO and humanitarian field workers who need affordable team safety tools, journalists and photographers working in complex environments, and missionaries and volunteers operating in regions without established safety infrastructure.

If the places you care about are scattered across the world, Travel Safe is for you.
